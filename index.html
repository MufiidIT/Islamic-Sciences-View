Islamic Sciences

Tawheed

The science of Tajweed focuses on strengthening one's understanding of how to declare the oneness of Allah - both in theory and practice.

'Aqeedah

The science of ‘Aqeedah consists of six articles of Eemaan; belief in Allah, His Angels, His Books, His Messengers, the Last Day and Destiny.

Arabic

The Arabic language is language of Islam and key to understanding its primary sources.

Arabic Sciences

There are 12/13 sciences of the Arabic language including:

  1. An-Nahw
  2. As-Sarf
  3. Ar-Rasm/Al-Imlaa-u
  4. Al-Inshaa'u
  5. Al-Bayaan
  6. Al-Badee'u
  7. Al-Ma-'aanee
  8. Qardush-shi'ir
  9. Al-'Uroodh
  10. Al-Qawaafee
  11. Al-Khataabah
  12. At-Taareekh
  13. Mutoon Al-Lughah
By mastering all of these science, it will aid the seeker of knowledge to understand the primary sources of Islam.

Fiqh

The science of Fiqh deals with knowledge of the Shar‘i rulings extracted from detailed evidence and proof.

Usul Al Fiqh

The science of Usul Al Fiqh plays a pivotal role for a seeker of knowledge to understand Fiqh. It enables one to understand extensive proofs, how to gain benefit from them, and the prerequisite conditions from the individual bring them out.

Tajweed

The science of Tajweed enables one to recite the Qur’aan in accordance of the way of the Prophet ﷺ, through studying the points of entry and properties of letters. This entails two sides; theory and practice.

Tafseer

The science of Tafseer allows one to understand the meaning of the words of Allah.

Hadith

Hadith is a science which has its own sciences. Hadith fundamentally is founded on rational guidelines, with a methodology based on an accumulation of experience attained by the hadith scholars on the basis of many years’ experience of narrating reports and checking their authenticity and soundness. Such knowledge was attained through a great depth of verifying reports, searching for them, sharing them, looking through them and ascertaining whether there were any faults in them, therefore progressively expanding the basic guidelines and structure of the science of hadith.

Tazkiyyah

The science of Tazkiyyah deals with purifying one's soul, and raising him/her upon true Islamic manners. This science is evidently crucial as the righteous predecessors would first learn manners, then knowledge.

Seerah

The science of Seerah is where one learns about the Prophet’s ﷺ life including his birth and death.

Tareekh

The science of Tareekh entails the social, economical, military, political and cultural progression of Islamic civilization post the death of the Prophet ﷺ.
